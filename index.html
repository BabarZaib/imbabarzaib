About Me

I am an Industrial & Manufacturing Engineer from NUST Karachi, graduating with a Gold Medal for academic excellence.

Over the years, I have expanded my expertise beyond engineering into Data Science, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI-driven solutions, supported by a Master's in Computer Science specializing in Machine Learning and Data Science.

With 8+ years of experience across supply chain, production planning, and product development, I now focus on building intelligent, data-centric solutions that blend my engineering foundation with modern AI capabilities. My passion lies in transforming traditional processes through analytics, automation, and scalable tech innovation.
